The Best Video Games I've Ever Played

My top 3 games of all-time.

Resident Evil 4

Notice how I didn't specify whether the remake or the original? I can't decide. Both games are beyond amazing, and are titles I have sunk hundreds of hours into.

Rainbow Six Siege

Legitimately, the worst and the best game of all time. That pretty much sums the game up really.

Yakuza 0

80s Japan, an engaging story and a kickass soundtrack. You really don't need much more
